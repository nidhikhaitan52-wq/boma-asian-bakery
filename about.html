About Boma Asian Bakery

Boma Asian Bakery is a Kolkata-based bakery bringing authentic Asian desserts to the city. Inspired by Japanese and Korean baking traditions, we focus on light textures, unique flavors like matcha and red bean, and beautifully crafted sweets.

Every item is freshly prepared using high-quality ingredients. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or desserts for special occasions, Boma offers a unique experience.
